==========================
Spack Tutorial CINECA 2026
==========================

3-day EasyBuild + EESSI + Spack tutorial/hackathon in Bologna (19–21 May 2026).
In-person training/hackathon on EasyBuild, EESSI, and Spack, organised in collaboration with MaX EuroHPC Centre of Excellence.

These pages cover the **Spack** portion of the agenda. The Spack sessions are:

* **Day 1, 14:00–15:30**: Introduction to Spack: environments and package recipes
  (:ref:`environments-tutorial`, :ref:`packaging-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 09:30–10:00**: Software Deployment CI/CD pipelines in GitLab with Spack
  (:ref:`stacks-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 11:30–12:00**: Spack-driven Development Workflow
  (:ref:`developer-workflows-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 12:30–13:00**: Containerization of software with Spack
  (:ref:`binary-cache-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 14:00–14:30**: Best practices for reproducible builds
  (:ref:`reproducible-builds-tutorial`)
* **Day 3**: Open hands-on session
