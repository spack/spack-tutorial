==========================
Spack Tutorial CINECA 2026
==========================

A three-day in-person training and hackathon on EasyBuild, EESSI, and Spack, held in Bologna from 19 to 21 May 2026 in collaboration with the MaX EuroHPC Centre of Excellence.

These pages cover the Spack portion of the agenda.
The Spack sessions are:

* **Day 1, 14:00–15:30**: Introduction to Spack: environments and package recipes (:ref:`environments-tutorial`, :ref:`packaging-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 09:30–10:00**: Software Deployment CI/CD pipelines in GitLab with Spack (:ref:`stacks-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 11:30–12:00**: Spack-driven Development Workflow (:ref:`developer-workflows-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 12:30–13:00**: Containerization of software with Spack (:ref:`binary-cache-tutorial`)
* **Day 2, 14:00–14:30**: Best practices for reproducible builds (:ref:`reproducible-builds-tutorial`)
* **Day 3**: Open hands-on session
