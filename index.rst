.. _spack-101:

==========================
Spack Tutorial CINECA 2026
==========================

A three-day in-person training and hackathon on EasyBuild, EESSI, and Spack,
held in Bologna from 19 to 21 May 2026 in collaboration with the MaX EuroHPC
Centre of Excellence.
These pages cover the Spack portion of the agenda.
For the main Spack documentation, see
`spack.readthedocs.io <https://spack.readthedocs.io>`_.

.. container:: day-card

   .. rubric:: Day 1: Introduction to Spack

   **14:00–15:30**
      Environments and package recipes
      (:ref:`environments-tutorial`, :ref:`packaging-tutorial`).

.. container:: day-card

   .. rubric:: Day 2: Advanced Topics

   **09:30–10:00**
      CI/CD pipelines in GitLab with Spack (:ref:`stacks-tutorial`).

   **11:30–12:00**
      Spack-driven development workflows (:ref:`developer-workflows-tutorial`).

   **12:30–13:00**
      Binary caches (:ref:`binary-cache-tutorial`).

   **14:00–14:30**
      Best practices for reproducible builds (:ref:`reproducible-builds-tutorial`).

.. container:: day-card

   .. rubric:: Day 3: Open Hands-on Session

   Unstructured time to work through tutorial material, experiment with Spack
   on your own projects, and ask questions of the instructors.
   Suggested exercises: :ref:`hackathon-tutorial`.
